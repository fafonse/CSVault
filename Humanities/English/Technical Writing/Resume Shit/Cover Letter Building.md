Header:
- Keep it the same as the [[Resume Building|resume]]
- Do not do “To whom it may concern”, or gendered vague salutations
    - Try to find out who the hiring manager/paper pusher is
    - Professional and perfect 🙂
    
Formatting:
- Full business block format
    - Keep a full space/line between paragraphs
    - No indents, single spaced with a double between
- Fill out the page
    - Bigger margins, double space, larger font, etc.
    - Bring the header down a bit
    
Introduction:
- Be eager about it
- Mention any internal contacts if there is one
    
Explanation:
- Explain why you’re fit for the job
- Why are you the *best* choice?
    - Eager to learn, young, hungry in a sense
    
Invitation:
- Invite them for a return communication
    - Don’t put in your schedule, if you’re busy let them email you
  
Valediction:

- The “Sincerely…” part of the letter    	
- Keep it professional, don’t get personal
	- “Kind/warm regards, “
	- “Sincerely,”
	- “Thank you for your time,”
- Leave space for a signature
    - Valediction $\rightarrow$ 3 or 4 spaces $\rightarrow$ Name in print
