The way that books interact with us and help us view our world.

## Mirrors

Books that children can see themselves and feel validated in their life. It helps children see their own value in the world and self-reflect.

## Windows
Books where children can look into someone else's world.

*Gives children empathy for others outside their common life.*

## Sliding Glass Door

Books that allow children to "step" into another world. A fully immersive experience, usually one where you can imagine yourself in the world.
