A forwarding table for [[Internet Protocol|IP]] and [[MAC address|MAC addresses]].

`< IP address; MAC address; TTL >`

The TTL determines the how long the address mapping with be in the table (~20 minutes).

> [!warning] Public WiFi
> Because public networks don't use authentication, this is why you have to worry about hackers. They could potentially see all of your data flowing in and out. This is also why we [[Cryptography#Types of Encryption|encrypt]] all of out info.
