An application-layer protocol on top of [[User Datagram Protocol|UDP]].

- Increased HTTP performance
- Deployed on many Google servers
- Includes a bit of encryption
- Uses [[Hyper Text Transfer Protocol|HTTP]]/3

Also includes TCP-like [[TCP Competition|congestion control]], as well as [[Handshaking|connection establishment]].

> Can have multiple app streams multiplexed over a single connection
