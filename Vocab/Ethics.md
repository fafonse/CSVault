## Aristotle
According to Aristotle, ethics in political discourse is a matter of Goodness and [[Utilitarianism|Utility]].
While in *Rhetoric* he seems to only care about the [[Ethos of Expediency|expediency]] of deliberate [[Rhetoric|rhetoric]], it can be argued that arguing about the [[Praxis|praxis]] is the means to the end of which a speaker uses [[Phronesis|phronesis]] to argue for it.
