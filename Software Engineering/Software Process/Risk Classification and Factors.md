Risk = Probability \* Consequence

Probability: What is the chance of occurrence?
Consequence: How serious is the impact?

When making an assessment of risk, you need to understand both probability and consequence to properly asses risk.

![](https://www.sentrient.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/5x5-Risk-Matrix-Example.jpg.webp)


## Probability Scale
1. Rare
2. Unlikely
3. Moderate
4. Likely
5. Almost certain

## Consequence Scale
1. Insignificant
2. Minor
3. Significant
4. Major
5. Severe
