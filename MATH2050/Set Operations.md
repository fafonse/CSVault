- Union $A\cup B$: *A* or *B* or both
	- `if (a or b)`
- Intersection $A \cap B$: *A* and *B*
	- `if (a and b)`
- Complement A': not *A*
	- `not(a)`
- Mutually exclusive: $A \cap B = \emptyset$
	- *A* and *B* never happen together
